Understand the fr Unit:
The fr unit divides the available space in the grid container into proportional parts. In 2fr 10fr 4fr, the total number of "fractions" is:
2 + 10 + 4 = 16

Calculate the Proportion of Each Column:
Each column's width is determined by dividing its fr value by the total number of fractions (16).

First Column (2fr):
(2 / 16) × 100 = 12.5%
The first column takes 12.5% of the total width.

Second Column (10fr):
(10 / 16) × 100 = 62.5%
The second column takes 62.5% of the total width.

Third Column (4fr):
(4 / 16) × 100 = 25%
The third column takes 25% of the total width.

Final Percentages
First Column: 12.5% of the container's width.
Second Column: 62.5% of the container's width.
Third Column: 25% of the container's width.
